# Developing AI Concierge for International Patients

**Date/Time:** June 18, 2026, 11:41 AM EDT

## Overall Summary

The discussion centers on creating a text-based AI concierge agent to assist international patients undergoing medical procedures abroad. The agent aims to handle low-severity inquiries autonomously, escalate higher-stakes issues to humans, and integrate with existing communication channels like iMessage. Key considerations include patient itinerary management, personalized guidance, data privacy, and regulatory compliance.

## Key Points

- Patients face logistical and medical concerns abroad, such as transportation issues and hospital procedures, requiring real-time support.
- The AI agent should manage routine questions (e.g., local dining, itinerary changes) while escalating critical matters to human staff.
- Data sources include patient itineraries, pre-op instructions, and past clinic communications, with challenges in integrating post-op personalized instructions.
- Privacy and regulatory compliance are critical; zero data retention agreements with API providers are in place to protect patient information.
- The development stack leverages iMessage and local AI processing on MacBooks, with testing facilitated via virtual phone numbers (Burner app).
