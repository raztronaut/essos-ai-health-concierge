# Rhinoplasty Pre-op Packet

This packet is derived from the provided Essos example itinerary and rhinoplasty pre-op source material. Eve may answer routine pre-op reference questions from this document when the answer is directly supported here.

## Smoking and Nicotine

Stop all nicotine products at least 2 weeks before surgery, including cigarettes, vapes, e-cigarettes, patches, and gum. Nicotine negatively affects healing and increases anesthesia risks.

## Alcohol

Avoid alcohol at least 1 week before surgery, as it increases the risk of bleeding.

## Food and Drink

Do not eat or drink anything, including water, at least 6-8 hours before your surgery. Have a light meal the night before and nothing after midnight.

## Medications

Avoid blood-thinning medications such as Aspirin, Ibuprofen, Naproxen, Voltaren, etc., at least 5-7 days before surgery, and consult your doctor first. Do not take any medication without informing us.

## Vitamins and Supplements

Stop all vitamins and herbal supplements at least 1 week before surgery, especially Vitamin E, fish oil, ginseng, ginkgo biloba, ginger, garlic supplements, and green tea extract, as they may increase bleeding risk.

## Health Condition

If you have any illness such as flu, cold, sore throat, fever, or infection, inform us immediately before your procedure.

## Facial Procedures

Avoid any additional facial treatments before surgery, such as fillers, Botox, PRP, skin treatments, or chemical peels.

## Personal Preparation On Surgery Day

Do not wear makeup, cream, perfume, or lotion. Do not wear nail polish, jewelry, or contact lenses. Men must be clean-shaven. Wear comfortable, loose clothing, preferably tops that open from the front.

## Physical Activity

Avoid heavy exercise and intense physical activity before the procedure.

## Consultation And Expectations

Make sure all your expectations and desired results are clearly discussed with your doctor before surgery.
