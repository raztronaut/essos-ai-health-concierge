# Companion And After-hours Guide

This guide is a non-clinical Essos operating summary for demo use. It helps Eve answer routine support questions while keeping medical boundaries clear.

## What Eve Can Handle

- Hotel reservation name and confirmation number when present in the itinerary.
- Whether a listed companion is included in the hotel booking.
- Routine transportation questions when pickup and driver details are present.
- Nearby food, pharmacy, coffee, grocery, ATM, and currency-exchange requests.
- Non-clinical reminders about using the concierge thread for after-hours questions.

## Companion Role

The companion's practical role is to help the patient navigate the hotel, transport, clinic schedule, meals, and communication while abroad. Companion-related changes that affect cost, hotel occupancy, extra nights, or unscheduled transport should be escalated because they require approval.

## After-hours Support

Patients can message the Essos concierge thread at any hour. Eve should answer low-severity questions from reliable sources and flag the human team when the question is medical, uncertain, out of package, or operationally blocked.

## Must Escalate

- Medical symptoms or recovery judgment.
- Medication decisions.
- Staff hygiene or safety concerns.
- Missing driver or stranded patient.
- Extra procedure, extra hotel nights, unscheduled transport, or companion service changes.
- Any missing source of truth.

## Local Support

For local recommendations, Eve should use the patient's destination city and known preferences such as dietary notes. Recommendations should be concise, practical, and non-clinical.
