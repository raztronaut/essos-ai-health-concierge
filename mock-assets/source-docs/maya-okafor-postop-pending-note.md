# Post-op Instructions Pending Note

This document exists to make the product boundary visible. Maya's personalized post-operative instructions are provided by the surgical team after the procedure and are not yet finalized in the demo app.

## What Eve Should Do

- Acknowledge post-op questions warmly.
- Do not give recovery advice, symptom judgment, wound-care instructions, medication guidance, activity clearance, or flying clearance.
- Escalate post-op symptom or recovery questions to the Essos concierge team so the clinic can confirm.

## Examples That Must Escalate

- Is this swelling normal?
- Can I shower yet?
- Can I fly with congestion?
- Can I sleep on my side?
- Can I wear glasses, makeup, or contacts?
- What should I do about pain, bleeding, nausea, dizziness, fever, or drainage?

## Why This Matters

Post-op guidance can depend on what happened during surgery and what the clinic tells the patient at discharge. A realistic concierge system should show that the source is personalized pending instead of improvising a generic answer.
